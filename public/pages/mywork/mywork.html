my work

✧✦ my art ✧

I'm a hobbyist artist. Digital art only. My illustrations have a really big focus in color, brush work and textures. Painting is my favorite part of the process, and what I consider is my biggest strength as well. I put hours of work and love and dedication in every single one of my pieces, and I feel like that's where it really shines through.

Most of my work is fan art. Specifically My Little Pony fan art, specifically the character Cozy Glow, as I've been deathly obsessed with her and the show for quite a few years now. But occasionally I draw other media and even my own characters.

I've also done a couple of commissions in the past. If you'd like to get one, make sure to check out my commissions page for TOS, prices and everything else you need to know.

Drawing is very important to me and It's something that's helped me through so many tough spots in life, helped me meet wonderful people and build a community online, and I just couldn't be more thankful to it.

✧✦ coding ✧

I'm also hobbyist programmer. Nowhere near where I'm at on my art journey. I know a good amount of HTML, CSS and some JS. All my coding experience comes from this website, a past scrapped version made for it, and some experiments here and there with Python, batch and a few game engines.

I don't consider myself an experienced programmer, and I do not do commissions or any freelance coding work and don't think I ever will. But if you have a question you think I might be able to answer, I'd be glad to help you out.

I see programming, specifically making this website and doing scripts for the few CLI applications I run, as... Something that makes life easier to deal with. This website is a way to express myself and I feel like a way to rebel against the current state of social media and the web in general, it's a safe space where I can voice my thoughts and be myself and not have to worry about all the other horrible things that entails anywhere else. I can't say the same lovely words about scripting however. Scripting to me isn't about self expression, it's about the same thing it is to everyone else, it makes a boring task faster to do or even automatic.
